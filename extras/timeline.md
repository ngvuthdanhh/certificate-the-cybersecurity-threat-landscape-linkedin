# Timeline of Major Cybersecurity Threats

| Year | Event | Impact |
|------|-------|--------|
| 2000 | ILOVEYOU Virus | Spread via email, infected millions worldwide |
| 2007 | Estonia Cyberattacks | First large-scale cyberattack on a nation-state |
| 2010 | Stuxnet Worm | Targeted Iranian nuclear facilities, first cyber-physical attack |
| 2013 | Target Breach | 40M credit card numbers stolen |
| 2017 | WannaCry & NotPetya | Ransomware & wiper attacks causing global disruption |
| 2020 | SolarWinds Attack | Supply chain compromise impacting U.S. government |
| 2021 | Colonial Pipeline | Ransomware disrupted U.S. fuel supply |
| 2022 | Log4j Vulnerability | Massive exploitation across global enterprises |
| 2023 | MOVEit Transfer Breach | Data theft affecting hundreds of organizations |
