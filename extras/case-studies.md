## Case Study 1: WannaCry Ransomware (2017)
- **Overview**: Global ransomware outbreak exploiting a Windows SMB vulnerability (EternalBlue).  
- **Impact**: Over 200,000 machines infected across 150+ countries. Major organizations (e.g., NHS UK) severely disrupted.  
- **Key Lessons**:  
  - Importance of timely patching.  
  - Backups and disaster recovery planning are critical.  

---

## Case Study 2: SolarWinds Supply Chain Attack (2020)
- **Overview**: Nation-state actors compromised SolarWinds Orion software updates.  
- **Impact**: Infiltration of U.S. government agencies and Fortune 500 companies.  
- **Key Lessons**:  
  - Supply chain security is a critical weak point.  
  - Zero Trust and strict monitoring of vendor software are essential.  

---

## Case Study 3: Colonial Pipeline Ransomware (2021)
- **Overview**: DarkSide ransomware disrupted fuel supply chain in the U.S.  
- **Impact**: Fuel shortages across the East Coast, panic buying, financial losses.  
- **Key Lessons**:  
  - Critical infrastructure is a prime target.  
  - Incident response and communication strategies must be prepared in advance.
