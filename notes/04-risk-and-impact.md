# Risk and Impact of Cyber Threats

Cyber threats can cause damage on multiple levels.  

### Types of Impact
- **Financial**: Direct theft, fraud, ransom payments, and recovery costs.  
- **Reputation**: Loss of customer trust and brand damage.  
- **Operational**: Business disruption and downtime.  
- **National Security**: Attacks on critical infrastructure, espionage, and cyber warfare.  

### Key Takeaways
- Cybersecurity is not just a technical issue but also a **strategic business and societal concern**.
