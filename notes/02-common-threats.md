# Common Threats in Cybersecurity

Cyber threats take many forms, ranging from technical attacks to social engineering.  

### Main Categories
- **Malware** – Viruses, worms, trojans that disrupt or steal information.  
- **Ransomware** – Encrypts data and demands payment for release.  
- **Phishing** – Deceptive messages designed to steal credentials or sensitive information.  
- **Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)** – Long-term, targeted campaigns usually backed by organized groups or nation-states.  
- **Insider Threats** – Risks from employees, contractors, or trusted third parties.  

### Key Takeaways
- Threat actors often combine multiple techniques.  
- Human error remains a critical factor in successful attacks.
