# The Future of Cybersecurity

The threat landscape will continue to evolve rapidly.  

### Emerging Trends
- **AI-Driven Attacks**: Automation and machine learning used by adversaries.  
- **Supply Chain Compromise**: Targeting third-party vendors to infiltrate organizations.  
- **Quantum Computing Risks**: Potential to break current cryptography in the future.  
- **Cybersecurity Workforce Demand**: Growing need for skilled professionals.  

### Key Takeaways
- The **arms race** between attackers and defenders will continue.  
- Continuous learning and adaptability are essential for professionals.
