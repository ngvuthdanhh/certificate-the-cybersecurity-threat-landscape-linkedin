# Index – The Cybersecurity Threat Landscape

This course provides an overview of today’s cybersecurity threat environment.  
Key modules covered include:

1. **Introduction to Threats** – Core concepts of threats, vulnerabilities, and risks.  
2. **Types of Threats** – Malware, ransomware, phishing, APTs, insider threats.  
3. **Attack Vectors** – Email, web, IoT, cloud, and mobile platforms.  
4. **Emerging Trends** – AI-driven attacks, supply chain compromises, zero-day exploits.  
5. **Risk and Impact** – The consequences of cyber threats for businesses, governments, and individuals.  
6. **Defense Strategies** – Defense-in-depth, threat intelligence, and incident response.  
7. **Future Outlook** – Anticipated trends and the evolving role of cybersecurity professionals.
