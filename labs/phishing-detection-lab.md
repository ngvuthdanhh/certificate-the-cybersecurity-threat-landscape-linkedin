# Lab: Phishing Detection

## Objective
Learn to detect and analyze phishing attempts through email and messaging platforms.

## Steps
1. Examine sample phishing emails (headers, links, sender information).  
2. Identify red flags such as spoofed domains, grammar mistakes, or urgent language.  
3. Use sandbox tools to safely inspect suspicious attachments or URLs.  
4. Document findings in a phishing incident report.  

## Deliverables
- A phishing red-flag checklist.  
- Example phishing report with evidence.  

## Key Learning
Phishing remains the most common entry point for attackers, making **awareness and detection** essential.
