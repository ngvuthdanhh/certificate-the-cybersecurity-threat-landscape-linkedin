# Lab: Risk Assessment Exercise

## Objective
Evaluate business risks from cyber threats and prioritize them for decision-making.

## Steps
1. List critical assets (customer data, financial systems, intellectual property).  
2. Identify threats that could impact these assets.  
3. Assign likelihood and impact scores.  
4. Plot risks on a **risk heat map**.  
5. Suggest mitigation strategies for high-priority risks.  

## Deliverables
- Completed risk matrix and heat map.  
- Recommended mitigation strategies.  

## Key Learning
Risk assessment provides a **structured approach** to securing valuable assets against cyber threats.
